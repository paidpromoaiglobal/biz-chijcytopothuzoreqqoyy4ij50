About Us

Adam Men's Salon in Krishna Nagar is Puducherry's go-to grooming haven for men who take their appearance seriously. Nestled at the corner of 15th Cross Road on Krishna Nagar Main Road in Lawspet, our salon combines a warm, welcoming atmosphere with highly skilled and professional staff who are dedicated to making every visit a relaxing, results-driven experience. With a stellar rating of 4.8 out of 5 from over 279 happy customers, we pride ourselves on delivering consistent quality every single time.

From precision haircuts and clean beard shaves to refreshing head massages and glowing skin facials, we offer a complete range of grooming services tailored to the modern Indian man. Our salon is open every day from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, ensuring you can always find a convenient time to look your best. Whether you're a daily regular or visiting for the first time, our friendly team guarantees a service that is professional, affordable, and absolutely worth your time.
